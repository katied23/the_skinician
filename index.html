Welcome to The Skinician!

In this evergrowing skincare industry, products seem endless and at times, very confusing.
Each skincare product is designed to target a specific skin type or skin issues, using the incorrect products for your skin can actually trigger or worsen the condition of your skin, yet it extremely common!
With this in mind, The Skinician aims to teach you the basics of skincare to suit your own skin whilst weeding out some products that are just unnecessary.
Our Skin Quiz will advise you of your skin type along with the basic products you need to start your skincare journey. Investing in your skin can be expensive, with that in mind your results will offer you a highend brand product along with a drugstore alternative. There are so many products available on the market so bear in mind, product recommendations are a guideline but you will be aware of what you need to search for in terms of products to get for your own skin needs.
